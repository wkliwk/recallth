Interaction Check
Checking Vitamin B12 against your cabinet
3 results found
MAJOR
Vitamin B12 + Metformin
Metformin is known to reduce Vitamin B12 absorption significantly. Long-term use of Metformin can lead to B12 deficiency. Consider monitoring B12 levels regularly with your doctor.
📚 Source: NIH Drug Interactions Database
MODERATE
Vitamin B12 + Vitamin D3
Taking both together is generally safe, but high doses of both fat-soluble vitamins may compete for absorption. Space intake by 2-4 hours for optimal absorption.
📚 Source: Mayo Clinic Reference
MINOR
Vitamin B12 + Probiotics
Probiotics may actually enhance B12 absorption. No negative interaction expected. Safe to take together.
📚 Source: PubMed Central
This information is for educational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making changes to your supplement or medication regimen.
